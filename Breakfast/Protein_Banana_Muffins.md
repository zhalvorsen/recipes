# Protein Banana Muffins

  - [Ingredients](#ingredients)

## Ingredients

- 3 overripe bananas
- 1 large egg
- 1/4 cup maple syrup
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 2/3 cup nonfat Greek yogurt
- 1 cup oats blended into flour
- 1/2 cup vanilla protein powder see notes
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 cup chocolate chips

## Directions

1. Preheat & Prep: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a 12-cup muffin tin with liners.
1. Mash & Mix: In a large bowl, mash your bananas until smooth. Stir in the egg, maple syrup, vanilla extract, and Greek yogurt.
1. Blend the Oats: Blend your oats into a fine flour using a blender or food processor (or cheat and use oat flour).
1. Combine Dry Ingredients: In a separate bowl, mix the oat flour, protein powder, baking powder, cinnamon, and salt.
1. Fold it Together: Gently fold the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients. Stir until just combined—don’t overdo it! Fold in the chocolate chips.
1. Fill & Bake the Healthy High Protein Banana Muffins: Divide the batter evenly between the 12 muffin liners. Pop them into the oven and bake the healthy high protein banana muffins for 25–30 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
1. Cool Down: Let the healthy high protein banana muffins cool in the tin for 5 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.
