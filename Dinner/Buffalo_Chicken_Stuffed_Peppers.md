# Buffalo Chicken Stuffed Peppers

[Source](https://www.thisfarmgirlcooks.com/buffalo-chicken-stuffed-peppers/#recipe)

- [Title](#title)
  - [Ingredients](#ingredients)
  - [Directions](#directions)

## Ingredients

- 3 multi-colored bell peppers halved lengthwise, and hollowed out
- 12 ounces cooked shredded chicken
- 3 ounces cream cheese softened
- 1/3 cup + 2 Tablespoons shredded cheddar cheese divided
- 1/4 cup + 2 Tablespoons diced green onion divided
- 1/2 cup buffalo sauce such as Frank's Hot Sauce
- 1 1/2 teaspoons ranch seasoning
- Ranch dressing for drizzling optional; not included in nutrition below.

## Directions

1. Preheat the oven to 375°F.
1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the chicken, cream cheese, 1⁄3 cup shredded cheese, 1⁄4 cup diced green onion, buffalo sauce, and ranch seasoning.
1. Stir until the ingredients are combined and chicken is fully coated in the buffalo sauce.
1. Scoop about 1⁄4-1⁄3 cup of the buffalo chicken mixture into a hollowed out pepper. Repeat with the other five halves.
1. Place stuffed peppers on a rimmed baking sheet. Sprinkle with the remaining 2 Tablespoons of cheese and cover with foil.
1. Bake for 45 minutes. Remove from the oven, plate and drizzle with ranch and sprinkle with diced green onion.
