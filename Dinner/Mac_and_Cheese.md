# Pressure Cooker Macaroni and Cheese

* 16 oz elbow macaroni
* 2 cups chicken stock
* 1 tsp salt
* 1 tsp pepper
* 1 Tbs butter
* 1.5 cup milk
* 1.5 cups shredded cheddar cheese
* 1.5 cups shredded mozerella

Pour pasta into pot with chicken broth. Pressure cook on high for 6 minutes Do a quick release. Place on saut√© mode and stir in other ingredients.
