# Spice Red Lentil Curry
[Source](https://minimalistbaker.com/spicy-red-lentil-curry/)

- 2 tbsp coconut oil
- 3 cloves garlic
- 1 tsp ginger
- 3 tbsp red curry paste
- 6 oz tomato paste sauce
- 2 cups chicken broth
- 2/3 cup lentils
- 1 sweet potato, diced
- 1/2 tsp curry powder
- 1 can coconut milk
