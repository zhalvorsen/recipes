- [Title](#title)
  - [Ingredients](#ingredients)

## Ingredients

- Pesto sauce
- Angel hair pasta
- Zucchini
- Cherry Tomatoes
- Kalamata Olives
- Artichokes
- Rotisserie chicken

## Directions

1. Chop all veggies and chicken
1. Sautee all veggies and chicken
1. Add some pesto
1. Mix some pesto with cooked noodles
1. Serve with sauteed mix on pesto noodles
