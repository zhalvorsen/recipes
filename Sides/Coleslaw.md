## Ingredients

### Salad

- 1 medium head of cabbage
- 2 medium carrots, grated

### Dressing

- 2 cups mayonnaise
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 1/4 cup dijon mustard
- 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
- 1 tsp salt
- 1/8 tsp pepper
