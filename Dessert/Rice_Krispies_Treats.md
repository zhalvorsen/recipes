# Rice Krispies Treats

[Snackable Bakes](https://www.amazon.com/Snackable-Bakes-Easy-Peasy-Exceptionally-Scrumptious/dp/1682687376)

## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup butter
- 1/3 cup sweetened condensed milk
- 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
- 1 teaspoon kosher salt
- 1 10-ounce bag of mini marshmallows, plus 1 cup
- 6 cups Rice Krispies cereal

## Directions

1. Grease 8-inch square cake pan
1. Melt butter in a large pot over medium to medium-high heat until it browns, smells nutty, and begins sputtering, 3-5 minutes. Stir in the sweetened condensed milk, vanilla, and salt with a flexible spatula until combined, and then 10 ounces of marshmallows until melted.
1. Off the heat stir in the cereal and remaining cup of marshmallows, until combined. Transfer to the prepared pan, spray hands with cooking spray or rub a little butter on them and flatten the mixture lightly and evenly.
1. Let the them set up on the counter for at least an hour.
